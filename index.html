An embassy for the curious

Tucked inside a historic two-story mansion on Sarmen Street, ПЭУ is Yerevan's warmest creative refuge. Wild grapevines wrap the walls. Daisy-shaped tabletops dot the courtyard. Pink tuff stone, marble, and glazed ceramics create a tactile backdrop that's unmistakably Armenian.

Multiple zones — an ambient bar on the first floor, a wine bar on the veranda, the main bar upstairs, and a billiards room — mean the energy shifts room to room. The garden breathes in summer. The lighting stays golden all year.

The vibe, by floor

ПЭУ was never meant to be just one thing. The mood rises as you climb. Start slow, end dancing — or stay in the garden all night.

Ground floor: ambient bar, soft lighting, ceramic tiles, slow drinks
Veranda: wine bar, orchard views, natural light, linen & stone
First floor: main bar, DJ booth, full energy, signature cocktails
Garden: courtyard, daisy tables, grapevines, fireflies in summer
Billiards room: when you want a game with your whisky
